# Atlas

The Atlas section will contain geographic visualisations related to executive elites in Latin America and Southern Europe.

This section is intended for choropleth maps and other spatial summaries. Future maps may display cross-national variation in executive recruitment, political experience, gender composition, partisan background, cabinet turnover, or other attributes of executive elites.

## Planned outputs

- Choropleth maps by country
- Regional comparisons
- Indicators of executive elite composition
- Cross-national summaries of political and administrative career patterns

{: .note }
Map outputs will be added once the underlying indicators and geographic layers are finalised.
