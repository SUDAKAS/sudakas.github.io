# Incumbents

The Incumbents section contains visual summaries of current governments.

This section focuses on the profile of current executive elites across several dimensions: partisan, political, occupational, managerial, academic, and typological. The aim is to provide a compact overview of who currently governs, how incumbents are positioned politically, and what types of professional and institutional backgrounds they bring to executive office.

## Planned outputs

### Partisan Dimension

Placeholder for plots on party affiliation, party families, government-opposition background, and partisan composition of incumbent governments.

### Political Dimension

Placeholder for plots on prior political experience, elected office, legislative background, party leadership, and previous executive roles.

### Job Dimension

Placeholder for plots on occupational and professional background before entering executive office.

### Managerial Dimension

Placeholder for plots on managerial, organisational, bureaucratic, or executive leadership experience.

### Academic Dimension

Placeholder for plots on educational credentials, academic experience, university background, and expert profiles.
